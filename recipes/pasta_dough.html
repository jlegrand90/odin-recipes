Pasta Dough

No fuss pasta dough

This pasta dough can be made 2-3 hours before you need it with adequate time for gluten to form and rest.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams flour
  • 5 eggs whole

Steps

  1. measure and weigh out your flour and create a well
  2. add your whole eggs to the well, and using a fork wisk flour slowly into a dough
  3. once dough has formed continue kneading with your hands untill fully incorporated
  4. you should see some push back when you press the dough, this shows that gluten has formed
  5. wrap tightly with plastic wrap and let rest for 30 minutes
  6. knead once more for about 5 minutes, and then rest another 30 min, or store in fridge overnight for production next day
  7. dough is now ready to roll out
