Saffron Stock

Base stock to be used in soups or sauces

Use this stock as a base for mussels, or use for creating flavor in paella etc

Ingredients

  • 1000 grams onion
  • 1000 grams tomato
  • 20 grams smoked paprika
  • 1 gram saffron
  • 5 liter veg stock

Steps

  1. Sweat onion until fragrant.
  2. Add tomato, smoked paprika, and saffron.
  3. Sweat until fragrant and dry.
  4. Add veg stock and simmer for 1 hour.
  5. Strain and transfer to containers, label and date.
