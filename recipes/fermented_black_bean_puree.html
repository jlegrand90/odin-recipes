Fermented black bean puree

This is an umami rich sauce that is great to add to roasted vegetables as a glaze, to give a bit of a depth of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams fermented black bean
  • 100 grams shallot
  • 50 grams garlic
  • 85 grams ginger
  • 280 grams red wine
  • 130 grams grapeseed oil
  • 85 grams water

Steps

  1. Rinse Fermented Black Bean under cold water untill water runs clear.
  2. Sweat shallot, ginger, garlic untill fragrent.
  3. Add fermented black beans, and deglaze with red wine.
  4. Reduce to almost sec. Add water and remove from heat.
  5. Blend in Vitamix, and emulsify grapeseed oil slowly.
  6. Transfer to pint containers, label and date.
